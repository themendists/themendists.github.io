About us

Behind the Music

Nakiso

Minimal dub techno.

Nocktik

Electronic.

The Mendists is a musical project by Nakiso and Nocktik, two artists who are pushing the boundaries of what Eswatini's music scene has ever seen. Their innovative approach to everything music-related, from their eclectic taste in music to their unique mixtapes and playlists, has garnered them a devoted following.

The Mendists produce a wide range of dance music, with each artist bringing their own distinct style to the table. Nakiso's minimal dub techno influences and Nocktik's background in EDM come together to create a sound that is truly one-of-a-kind. Their latest release, Artifacts, is a testament to their ability to blend their individual art forms into something truly special.

In addition to their music, The Mendists are known for their visually stunning live performances and their top-quality merchandise. With their boundless creativity and unrelenting dedication to their craft, it's no wonder that The Mendists are quickly becoming one of the most buzzed-about acts in the industry.
